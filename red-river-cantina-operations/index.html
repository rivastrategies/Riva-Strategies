BRAND FOUNDATION Concept Positioning Red River Cantina is a social, energetic, welcoming Tex-Mex restaurant concept focused on drinks, atmosphere, entertainment, food, and community familiarity. The brand should feel active, local, consistent, authentic, and experience-driven. Core Essence: Modern Mexican cantina. Coastal Texas Tex-Mex. Vibrant food, handcrafted drinks, and warm hospitality in an energetic, festive atmosphere.  Events are amplification — not the foundation. Normal daily operations, Happy Hour visibility, social atmosphere, drinks, entertainment, and customer familiarity are the true long-term drivers. Cinco de Mayo is a volume booster, not the content strategy. STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES SOP Reference Cards Each SOP defines the execution standard for a specific content or operational function. These are the operating rules — not suggestions. 01 Daily Story SOP Stories run every day. Prioritize Happy Hour, drinks, atmosphere, food, patio, and entertainment. Build native for vertical viewing. Authentic content over polished graphics. Frequency increases on Fri/Sat and event days. DAILY HIGH PRIORITY REAL MEDIA ONLY 02 Wall Post SOP Wall posts maintain public-facing visibility. Not every day needs one — but the rhythm must stay consistent. Cover Happy Hour, food, drinks, atmosphere, events, and community. Keep it clean and mobile-friendly. 3–5X WEEKLY CONSISTENT CADENCE 03 Happy Hour Visibility SOP Happy Hour gets dedicated visibility multiple times per week. Priority days: Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat. Show drinks, bar atmosphere, patio energy, and specials. Feels ongoing and social — never like a coupon ad. TOP TRAFFIC DRIVER TUE–SAT STORIES + WALL 04 Week Ahead SOP Week Ahead content is posted early in the week to set expectations and prime visibility. Covers upcoming specials, entertainment, events, and recurring promotions. Helps customers plan ahead and reinforces routine. MONDAY PUSH PLANNING TOOL 05 Event Amplification SOP Events amplify the existing system — they don't replace it. Pre-event announcements, countdown stories, real-time atmosphere content, and post-event recaps. Always transition back to normal cadence after an event wraps. AMPLIFICATION ONLY PRE + LIVE + RECAP 06 Cross-Posting SOP High-value content gets shared across both location pages. Always verify location accuracy, adjust captions when needed, and avoid over-duplication. Strategic use only — not automatic syndication. BOTH LOCATIONS STRATEGIC USE ONLY 07 Reel & Video SOP Video content should capture real atmosphere, real energy, and real customer experience. Short-form, mobile-optimized, authentic. Not polished brand reels. Not stock footage. Not manufactured social moments. REAL FOOTAGE ONLY MOBILE-FIRST 08 Community Engagement SOP Monitor tags, comments, and shares. Respond consistently. Highlight positive customer moments. Support local events and partnerships when brand-aligned. Keep all public responses welcoming and professional. ONGOING LOCAL CONNECTION 09 Visual Standards SOP Real food. Real drinks. Real atmosphere. Real customer activity. Brand colors, typography, and layout rules must be followed exactly. No AI imagery. No fake architecture. No clip art. No hard-to-read text. NON-NEGOTIABLE BRAND KIT REQUIRED 10 Website Update SOP Website should always match social. Specials, Happy Hour, events, hours, and menus stay current. Remove expired promotions immediately. Mobile readability is required. Consistency between web and social builds customer trust. CONTENT GUARDRAILS Anti-AI Formatting Guidance The Cantina system has hard rules against artificial or misleading content. These apply to all content types, all platforms, and all operators. No AI-generated food or drink imagery — ever. It misrepresents the real product. No fake or altered building architecture — use real photos of the actual locations only. No overproduced graphics that make the Cantina feel like a corporate chain. No manufactured social moments — real customer energy, real atmosphere. No trend-chasing content that lacks operational purpose or brand alignment. No nightclub-style marketing unless specifically event-driven and approved. No inaccurate pricing, times, or event information in any content. No low-quality, blurry, or outdated media in any post or story. SYSTEM RULES Non-Negotiables THESE CANNOT BE COMPROMISED. OPERATOR WORKFLOW Social Operations Workflow This section defines how operators and managers interact with the social operations system on a day-to-day basis. → Start of week: Pull the Week Ahead Review what specials, events, and entertainment are scheduled. Set the content calendar for the week. Don't improvise daily. → Daily check: Verify before publishing Always confirm current specials, times, and event details are accurate before any story or wall post goes live. → Media: Pull from the current library Use only approved, current real photography. If the library needs updated content, flag it and schedule a capture session. → Community: Monitor and respond consistently Check tags, comments, and messages. Respond promptly. Keep tone welcoming and professional — not corporate or scripted. → Events: Execute the amplification protocol For any major event, follow the Event Amplification SOP. Begin pre-event visibility early. Capture real-time content during the event. Post recap content after. → Website: Keep it synced with social Any specials, event listings, or hours posted on social should match the website exactly. Remove expired promotions immediately. → Escalation: Flag anything that needs approval New pricing, significant promotions, large event partnerships, or any off-brand content requests get reviewed before publishing. MONETIZATION FRAMEWORK Revenue Support Cadence Visibility content builds familiarity. Revenue content captures intent. Both systems must coexist inside the same operational cadence without either one displacing the other. The Cantina brand should never feel like it's selling — but it should always be converting. VISIBILITY CONTENT Builds familiarity over time. Atmosphere, Happy Hour, food, drinks, entertainment. The engine of long-term customer behavior. REVENUE CONTENT Captures intent when it exists. Delivery, direct ordering, packs, catering. Integrated — never the lead. COEXISTENCE RULE Both systems run simultaneously. Revenue content layers into existing windows. It does not replace cadence posts. AUTHENTICITY STANDARD Never sacrifice brand feel for conversion pressure. No fake urgency. No CTA stacking. No AI-generated marketing energy. Revenue content should feel like a natural extension of the atmosphere — not a departure from it. If a post reads like an ad, it's not right. 🛵 DoorDash / Delivery Visibility Maintain convenience-order awareness and support off-premise revenue without making the brand feel like a takeout operation.
