01

Objective

Create a consistent weekly system that:

  • Drives traffic ahead of time
  • Reaches new customers (non-followers)
  • Gives returning customers a clear weekly plan
02

Content Structure

Post Types
  • Feed Posts (FB + IG) — built for non-followers and traffic
  • Pinned Post — weekly reference for what's happening
  • Stories — daily reinforcement and live energy
Post Timing Windows
  • Midday posts — capture lunch and afternoon decisions
  • Evening posts (6–8 PM) — drive next-day traffic
  • Stories — daily, no gaps

09

How This Works

  • Sunday Midday Capture delivery demand via DoorDash / online ordering push
  • Sunday Night Set the weekly plan with the preview post
  • Monday Midday Lock the reference — pin the weekly preview
  • Monday Night Drive Tuesday traffic with the special push
  • Saturday Midday Capture family and group dining decisions
  • Saturday Night Show proof and energy to close the week strong
10

Important Note

Master Framework

Cantina locations will follow a consistent structure. BBQ locations will operate within the framework but remain flexible. Locations will be separated and refined after rollout.
